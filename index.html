About us

1 - Who we are

We are a Lebanese Uniform Factory based in Quennabet broumana.
We are known for our keen attention to detail and our punctuality.
Here, you can rest assured that everything is sewn with love.

At our factory, we sew school uniforms and work uniforms.
We manufacture all components of uniforms, including:
  • T-shirts
  • Ufo
  • Polo
  • Pants
  • Shirts
  • Apron waist
  • Apron neck
  • Jacket chef

  • Jackets
  • Etc.
